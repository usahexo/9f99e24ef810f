  1. glitch your way into the Casino.
  2. glitch your way out of the Casino with the Luchador Crib.

To glitch your way into the Casino, drive a car up to the shutter doors and park it so that it’s half in and half out of the building. Get out of the car and run towards the casino, but jump off the car before you reach it. You should now be inside the casino.

All you need to know about the Saints Row: The Third Monster Bird Game

The Saints Row: The Third Monster Bird Game is an open world action-adventure video game that was developed by Volition and published by THQ. It was released for the PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360 in November 2011, and for Microsoft Windows in January 2013. The game is the third installment in the Saints Row series, following 2008’s Saints Row 2.

Set a few years after the events of Saints Row 2, the Third Street Saints have become national celebrities thanks to their tower defense game show franchise “Steelport Hereos”. But when the Earth’s alien overlord Zinyak attacks the city, the Saints are kidnapped and transported to his simulated reality world.

In order to escape, players must fight through countless enemies while descending through four different levels of the simulation. Along the way they’ll unlock new abilities and powerful weapons to help them take down Zinyak and his army of digital henchmen.

Saints Row: The Third Monster Bird Game is a fun, over-the-top action game that provides hours of entertainment. The humor is sophomoric but very funny, and there’s a ton of things to do including stronghold takeovers, races, missions, stunts, etc. While it doesn’t push boundaries or innovate in any way, it does what it does very well and should appeal to fans of games like Grand Theft Auto.
